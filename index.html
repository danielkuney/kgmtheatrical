General Manager — New York

Broadway is changing.
Someone should say so.

I'm Daniel Kuney. I've spent more than two decades managing Broadway and Off-Broadway productions — the budgets, the contracts, the opening nights, and the weeks that don't go as planned.

I take on a small number of projects at a time, and I work them myself. If you hire me, you get me — not a junior staff member working from my notes. I've chosen to work this way because I think it produces better outcomes for my clients. A general manager who knows your show as well as you do is worth more than a firm that manages forty shows at once.

I also write, because I think this industry benefits from people who will say hard things clearly.
